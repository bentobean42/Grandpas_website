Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about our services.

What type of furniture can be refinished and what type can't?
All solid wood can be restored, any furniture that is either veneer or cheaper grade, it's a lot harder, but the look can be altered.
Can I change my painted cabinets into stained cabinets?
Yes, it is possible, but I will evaluate and see if it's cost-effective.
Can stained cabinets be painted?
Yes, it can without a lot of sanding.
How long do kitchen cabinets usually take?
On average, one to two weeks.
Is spraying or brushing better?
Sprayed looks factory finished but has its drawbacks. For example, any damage can be easily done, whereas a brushed look is 100% material on surfaces and a lot easier to touch up. I specialize in brush services with hardly any brush marks but will spray if desired. I prefer to brush.
